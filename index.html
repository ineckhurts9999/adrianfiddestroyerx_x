Moderator Guidelines & Protocols
General Conduct
01
Don't power abuse. (Do not mute, ban, kick, or warn members without a valid reason.)
02
Do not approve members who are sensitive or fail to provide enough information or context. (Doing so may result in losing your moderator role.)
03
If a member says they cannot send proof because Discord doesn't allow it, instruct them to slightly blur the image or cover parts of it while keeping the important details visible.
04
Members who troll or do not take the application process seriously must be banned. Please remove trolls to prevent issues for yourself and the server.
05
Any alternate accounts of a member, including kicked or banned users who keep rejoining, should be removed — except for approved staff accounts. (e.g., L, 413x, Skid)
06
If you decide to deny an application due to missing information, ask the member to reapply and ensure they provide all required details.
